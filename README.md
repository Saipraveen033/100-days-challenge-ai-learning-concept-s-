# 🚀 100 Days of AI

Welcome to my **#100DaysOfAI** journey! 👋

I'm documenting my journey of learning **Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning**—one concept at a time, every single day.

Instead of rushing through tutorials, I want to truly understand how AI works. This repository is my personal learning journal where I'll share simple notes, visual explanations, examples, and resources that help me learn. If you're also beginning your AI journey, I hope these notes make learning a little easier.

---

## 🎯 Why I'm Doing This

As a Computer Science student, I want to build a strong foundation in AI instead of just using AI tools.

My goals for this challenge are to:

* Learn one AI/ML concept every day
* Stay consistent for 100 days
* Build practical knowledge, not just theory
* Create projects based on what I learn
* Share my learning publicly and help others

> **"Small progress every day leads to big results."**
